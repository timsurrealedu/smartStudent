# Product

## Register

product

## Users

University students managing classes, assignments, schedules, notes, grades, and imported LMS data across desktop and phone. They use it between classes, while studying, and when checking deadlines from BINUSMAYA.

## Product Purpose

SmartStudent centralizes student academic life: courses, tasks, schedules, grades, notes, kanban work, and study planning. Success means a student can quickly see what matters today, import LMS data without friction, and trust that their personal school data is separated by account.

## Brand Personality

Clear, calm, practical. The app should feel like a focused academic cockpit: reliable enough for deadlines, light enough for daily use, and not overly corporate.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-site theatrics, decorative dashboards, oversized hero sections, and confusing custom controls. Avoid designs that bury task actions behind playful UI or require desktop-only workflows for phone users.

## Design Principles

- Mobile first for student reality: phone workflows must be first-class, especially import and daily review.
- Make data provenance obvious: imported BINUSMAYA content should be previewable and editable before it changes the account.
- Keep actions familiar: standard buttons, tabs, forms, and lists beat novelty.
- Respect privacy boundaries: each account owns its own academic data, and LifeOS remains separate/private.
- Prefer fast recovery: empty, error, and partial-import states should explain the next useful step.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and touch targets of at least 44px. Support reduced motion, readable mobile layouts, keyboard navigation for desktop users, and clear status text for import errors and successes.
